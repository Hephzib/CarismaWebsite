RADA ( Raise and Develop Association )

Carisma's Women at Workplace program RADA (Raise & Develop Association), is more a movement than a mere formality. This movement, now part of the company's DNA was initiated and curated by Jonah Stephen. Constituting about 47% of the work force, Carisma's vibrant team of professional women weave out well-planned activities, round the year, that energises, engages not only the women at work, but everyone in the organisation. Flagship initiatives include health & wellness training, leading women leaders talk, women safety and social awareness programs. And yes, a liberal dose of fun & entertainment too, at work keeps them empowered all the time!

Yoga

We at Carisma believe that good health is very important in life. Only when a person is in good health he can focus on work and also be successful in life. Practising yoga helps achieve this and also increases our concentration and reduces the stress of work.

To support this we have oraganised fitness training classes for our employees. Preethi Athreya visits us twice a month to teach us the right postures & yoga to be fit. She also explains to us the benefits yoga brings to each part of our body.

We also have Mr R Natarajan a reowned athlete and nine times National Record holder in sprints come to our office and organize sessions on fitness. He is also the director of Indian Sports Promotion Academy and many of our employees joined the academy for fitness training to get benefitted out of it.
