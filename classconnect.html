360° SMSF Accounting & Audit Services

For more than 12 years, Carisma has delivered comprehensive accounting services in Australia. Since 2012, Carisma has worked closely with Accounting firms to integrate Companies, Trust, Partnerships and individuals from other Business Service software such MYOB AE, MYOB AO, XPA, Handisoft etc. to Xero.

At Carisma, we integrate Xero with multiple software towards Reporting, Payroll, Client Management, Cloud document storage and Auto leads for data entry (see below for software details).

By integrating Carisma Solutions and XERO in your practices, you are getting a trusted financial accounting partner and business service solution using Australia's leading cloud-based financial software.
