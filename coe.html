Our Center Of Excellence ( COE ) for Accounting & Automation

Our Center of Excellence for Accounting & Automation fosters innovation, create software, develop skills and build best practices in the field of Accounting.

It develops solutions to increase productivity and security for accounting (KPO), helps evolve client relationship management framework to build strategic business alignment and long term partnership. The COE runs fresher’s training, deep skills training, leadership training and Special Interest Group.

Our RPA team is focussed in identifying areas for automation, developing solutions that impact business outcomes. We work closely with our clients and delivery centers to build solutions and implement them in our Client's Accounting Firm.
