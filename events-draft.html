Center of Excellence ( COE )

Our Center of Excellence (COE) for accounting and financial planning is fostered to develop skills and best practices in accounting and paraplanning. We have developed a body of knowledge / best practices in implementing Class and have been helping our clients with migration into Class software.

We have dedicated Class champions who can talk you through the app, its functionalities and what benefits it would bring to your accounting practice. Our staff are also highly knowledgeable in other apps that can link to your Class to help enhance your cloud accounting experience.
