Giving starts here

As part of our giving initiative, we are delighted to launch this page to encourage contributions to charitable organizations around the globe.

Currently, the Covid19 pandemic in India has caused stress on the country’s healthcare. The nation sees families stretching themselves financially, physically, and emotionally to support their loved ones and keeping them safe and nourished.

Your donations can help bring back lost smiles.

We invite contributions to these curated organizations providing essential supplies such as medicines, food, and shelter to the affected for Covid19.

Today, we actively support causes such as 1) Skill development 2) Work-Life-Balance for working women through RADA (Raise And Develop Association) 3) Support Self Help Groups (SHG) with our accounting solutions.
