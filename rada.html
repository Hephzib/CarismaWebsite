Our Founder

It is exciting to initiate, host and support RADA in Carisma Solutions since 2017. Working women across the globe face many challenges at work and are constantly challenged to manage themselves between work and family, career and responsibilities, finance and resources, time and fitness-health, vacation and school holidays, and much more.

RADA will continue to foster dialogue and discussion, build awareness, enable knowledge sharing in a fun-filled way among working women. It will work towards helping them manage their work-life balance better each day. Strive to help them to realize their aspirations at work and in the family. Provide the platform to get inspiration and inputs from those who have walked the path with all its trials and tribulations. I wish RADA to grow and enable working women of all ages and class to have improved work life balance across all industries.

Jonah Stephen
