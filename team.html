Jonah Stephen

CEO and Managing Director

A Management consultant with 27 years of experience in software and KPO industry in India, UK, USA and Indonesia. He worked in Microsoft as Chief Compete Officer, Director & Architect Advisor. Assistant Vice President in Polaris Financials Technology Ltd. And Technology Director in Kumaran Systems Inc., before starting to buildout Carisma he was the Cofounder and VP in Indix Corporation. He is the Executive Vice President of the Indo-Australian Chamber of Commerce. He is the custodian of the culture and values of the organisation. His leadership has enabled the business grow successfully and responsibly from being a start-up to a leading business in the industry today.



Muthuswamy

Chief Operating Officer

Muthuswamy is a leader in Global IT services delivery management. He is passionate about Software project management and business excellence. He is graduate from Vellore Institute of Technology and Stanford University majoring in EPMO. He is passionate about delivering exceptional customer service and building worldclass teams. Before joining Carisma, he served as a Director at Cognizant Technology Services. Prasoon Veerath

Group Director

Australia & New Zealand

A member of CPA Australia. Faculty member of the Institute of Public Accountants of Australia & the institute of Cost Accountants of India. He has over 13 years of experience in leading teams, building new services and delivering accounting services to Australia and New Zealand. He heads the Australia and New Zealand business. He is passionate about building new services and coaching freshers to success in the organisation.



Muthusubramanian

CFO & Advisor

Muthusubramanian is the group Chief Financial Advisor since 2014. He worked as Senior Vice President of Polaris Software Lab and other MNC companies for over 35 years. He is a qualified Company Secretary, Cost Accountant and a Graduate in Law. He is experienced in setting up India and overseas entities, managing finance, accounts, treasury, internal audit and statutory audits across countries. He is passionate about driving good corporate governance and practicing sound business ethics in the company.



Geetha Srikant

Business Advisor

A Chartered Accountant (India) & CPA (Australia) by qualification, Geetha is a passionate leader in Self Managed Super Fund. Her earlier experience was in the manufacturing sector as well as in accounting and audit practice. She is therefore well placed to provide virtual services to accounting firms anywhere in the world – Australian practice being her forte. At Carisma, Geetha advices the CEO and the board in growing the business and enabling client success.



Malathi MK

Business Advisor

A professional accountant, Malathi brings her experience of working with MNCs to build processes and migrate jobs flawlessly. She has over 15 years of corporate experience in manufacturing and other sectors, assisting corporate boards in budgeting, new projects and evaluating business proposals. As co owner and director at BOS , she expanded its operations to include new business segments. She headed their Investment portfolio reporting division. She was a sought after migration specialist for all accounting and administration assignments. At Carisma, Malathi advices the CEO and the board in growing the business and enabling client success. She is passionate about training and mentoring talent to build a strong stable of skilled accountants.
